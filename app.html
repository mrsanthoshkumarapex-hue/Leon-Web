About I Resume

I Resume is a smart and easy-to-use resume builder application designed to help users create professional resumes within minutes. Whether you are a student, fresher or experienced professional, the application guides you step-by-step to create clean and modern resumes without any design skills. Features include ATS-friendly templates, PDF export support, offline functionality and easy editing tools.
